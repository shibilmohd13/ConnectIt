Client Communication Portal

A client communication portal is a platform that allows a company to communicate with its clients or customers. This type of portal could be used to provide support, answer questions, or simply keep clients informed about the company's products or services.

''
Our final year project is a web application designed to streamline communication between clients and company employees. The application allows clients to send emails to a central email address, which are then received and analyzed by the application. Based on the contents of the email, the application will route the message to the appropriate employee for handling. The employee can then log in to the application and reply to the client directly, all from within the application. This eliminates the need for employees to constantly check their personal email accounts for client inquiries, and ensures that all communication is centralized and easily accessible. With this application, companies can improve the efficiency and effectiveness of their client communication processes, ultimately leading to better customer satisfaction and retention.
''





Our Client Communication Portal have the following features:

  • A messaging system that allows clients to send messages or request assistance from the company. This includes a simple form for clients to fill out, as well as the ability for the company to respond to and track incoming messages.A simple interface for clients to enter their name, email address, and the message they wish to send.
  • A backend system for employees to view, respond to, and track incoming messages. This includes a dashboard that displays all new and outstanding messages, with the ability to filter or search for specific conversations.
  • A messaging interface for employees to communicate with clients. This includes a text editor for composing responses, as well as the ability to attach files or links to the message.
  • A system for tracking the status of each message (e.g. "Pending", "Unread", "Read"). This could allow employees to see at a glance which messages need attention and which have already been resolved.
  • A customer database that stores contact information and past communication history for each client. This could allow employees to easily reference past conversations and personalize their responses.
  • The Application ensures the privacy of the company data. That is, Features such as Auto logout, cache control, auto reloding, data cleanup will provide extra protection to company data.
